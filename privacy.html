Jewish History Explorer

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

In short: Jewish History Explorer collects no data, creates no accounts, makes no network requests, and requests no browser permissions. Everything runs locally in your browser.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. There is no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party code.

Network requests

The app makes no network requests. All code, map data and historical data are bundled with the app and loaded locally. It works fully offline.

Local storage

The app saves a single flag in your browser's localStorage to remember that you've seen the one-time welcome tutorial, so it doesn't show again. This never leaves your device and you can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

Permissions

As a Chrome extension, it requests no permissions beyond opening its own page when you click the toolbar icon.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data at all, it is safe for users of any age.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated date above will change with it. Material changes will be noted on this page.
