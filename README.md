# Jewish History Explorer — live site

The public landing page and web app for **Jewish History Explorer**, a [42-apps](https://42-apps.github.io/) mini-site.

- **Live:** https://42-apps.github.io/jewishhistory/
- **Launch the app:** https://42-apps.github.io/jewishhistory/app/jewishhistory.html

An interactive globe and a **dual-track timeline** that run Jewish history along two rails — the
archaeological **record** beside the **tradition** — from ancient Mesopotamia to today. Each country is
shaded by its **Jewish population** over time; arcs trace the great migrations; ~400 events open to show
what the evidence shows and what scripture recounts, side by side, with a scholarly verdict.

Evidence-forward and even-handed. Figures are approximate, illustrative scholarly estimates; the map uses
modern national borders at every era as a visualisation device.

Source & the Chrome extension: a private companion repo. Borders: Natural Earth (public domain). Globe:
[globe.gl](https://github.com/vasturiano/globe.gl). Data: DellaPergola / American Jewish Year Book, Pew
Research, Jewish Virtual Library, Encyclopaedia Judaica, and the archaeological & documentary record.
